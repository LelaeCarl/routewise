# Product Overview – RouteWise

## 1. Product Vision

RouteWise is a web-based system that helps users choose the best logistics route between China and Kenya using intelligent algorithms.

The system simplifies complex logistics decisions into clear, actionable outputs.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Cross-border logistics is difficult because:

- Multiple transport modes exist
- Cost and time vary significantly
- Route planning is not intuitive
- Small traders lack decision tools

Users struggle to answer:
- What is the cheapest route?
- What is the fastest route?
- What is the best trade-off?

---

## 3. Solution

RouteWise solves this by:

- Modeling logistics as a graph
- Applying shortest-path algorithms
- Allowing user-defined preferences
- Displaying clear route recommendations

---

## 4. Target Users

- Small import/export traders
- E-commerce sellers
- Students and researchers
- Academic evaluators

---

## 5. Core Features

### 5.1 Route Planning
Users input:
- origin
- destination
- weight
- preference

System outputs:
- optimal route
- cost
- time

---

### 5.2 Multi-Modal Routing
Supports:
- Sea
- Air
- Rail
- Road

---

### 5.3 Optimization Modes
- Cheapest
- Fastest
- Balanced

---

### 5.4 Two-Way Logistics
- China → Kenya
- Kenya → China

---

### 5.5 Route Comparison
Displays multiple route options and trade-offs.

---

### 5.6 Knowledge Base
Provides information about:
- Ports
- Airports
- Logistics hubs

---

## 6. Product Goals

- Practical usefulness
- Academic correctness
